About

WatSFiC is the University of Waterlooâ€™s Science Fiction and Fantasy club. With roots in promoting interest in science fiction writing, the club has expanded to include all things science fiction, fantasy, and TTRPG. The club hosts many games of D&D 5e, Pathfinder, and Call of Cthulhu for all levels of players from beginner to master along with lesser known TTRPG systems. There are always books and movies to talk about or something to play!

Events

Dungeons & Dragons

WatSFiC runs a variety of D&D events each semester, including intro games for beginners, epics, tournaments, and 24 hour marathons. Check our calendar for dates and signup information! We welcome players of all experience levels. If you would like to volunteer as a Dungeon Master for one of our events, make sure to watch our news tab for new opportunities, our join our community on Discord.

Movie Nights

WatSFiC screens movies and tv shows, both in-person and online. In the past, we've done a Halloween movie night, and followed the tv shows House of the Dragon and The Last of Us. See our calendar for the dates and locations of in-person events. For movie/series discussions and online watch parties, check out our Discord.

Hangouts

Come out to our weekly hangouts on Friday nights, from 6-9pm in MC 2035. These events are free, casual, and drop-in. Try out a new board game or join one of our TTRPG groups.

Services

Library & Board Game Collection

WatSFiC maintains a library of board games and science fiction and fastasy books. They are available for signout in the MathSoc office

TTRPG Connect

Looking to join a Dungeons & Dragons group? Looking for players for your next Call of Cthulhu campaign? Join our Discord community of TTRPG players. Game Masters post advertisements looking for players for their adventures. You can find a variety of TTRPG systems, styles of play, and levels of experience.

Discounts

WatSFiC members recieve discounts at local game stores.
